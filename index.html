Newton's Law of Cooling

Differential Equation

$$\frac{dT}{dt} = -k(T(t) - T_{env})$$

Solution

$$T(t) = T_{env} + (T_0 - T_{env})e^{-kt}$$

Variable Definitions

$T(t)$: Temperature at time $t$
$T_{env}$: Ambient (environmental) temperature
$T_0$: Initial temperature at $t = 0$
$k$: Cooling constant (positive value)
$t$: Time (in minutes)
